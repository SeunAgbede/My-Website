About

Hi, I’m Seun Agbede a graphic designer and frontend developer living in London. I’m also the Co-founder of Bubble Network - a boutique digital agency offering creative solutions to SME’s across the UK. I’m currently at MSI UK helping to create amazing customer experiences. I enjoy solving problems and figuring things out with technology.

I play a lot of football and you're bound to catch me at your local park having a kickabout with my mates. Whenever I have time on my hands, I like to read books with themes centered around science and human behaviour. My favourite author - by a mile - is Yuval Noah Harari.

My other hobbies include running (Ruislip Running Club member - See my activities), playing the acoustic guitar, drawing and hiking at National Parks. I'm also very passionate about seeing Nigeria fulfil her potential and often express my thoughts through my Medium.
